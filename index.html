What is karrot?

karrot is a new Discord bot that is developed by kemosaf! It is still being developed and it might have few bugs. So far the bot only has around 50 commands, these will increase, I am planning to make 100! Right now, most of those commands are for fun, the bot is also equipped with an economy system, the bot is also customizable and you can change its prefix.
