Getting Started With Karrot!

To get started invite karrot by clicking the invite button below or by clicking invite on the navigation bar, once you invite Karrot to your server, try sending k!help this will give you the full list of commands, try them! If for some reason you don't like the default prefix, k! you can change it just for your server by running k!prefix 'your new prefix here'. That's pretty much it to get started! Have fun with Karrot and play around with it a little.
